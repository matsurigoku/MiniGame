A Speed Typing Game game with random sentences on the test questions, a total of 10 questions in one test.

After answering the questions, your test score will be displayed, with a full score of 10,000. The score will decrease with the time you spend.

You can also go to Rank to check your ranking.

This game allows you to add or remove questions by yourself, and these custom questions will also appear randomly in your questions.
